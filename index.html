About

A foundation built on precision, patience, and craft.

My career began on the hangar floor at Airworks Aviation, where I learned that aviation safety lives in the smallest details. That same discipline carried me through five years in Japan with the Churitsu Group — operating bending, end-forming, and auto-brazing machines that produce air-conditioning pipes for the automotive industry.

Working under the Technical Intern Training Program (TITP) shaped not just my technical skill set but how I approach work entirely: with consistency, accountability, and a deep respect for process. I'm now ready to bring that craftsmanship — and the cross-cultural fluency that comes with it — to my next chapter.
